Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

AI Work Orders ("we", "our", "the Service") is operated by Prompted To Do This, LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and password (encrypted) used to create your account.
  • Company information: Company name, phone, industry, and address you provide.
  • Work order data: Customers, technicians, services, and invoice records you create in the Service.
  • QuickBooks data: When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, we access only the data necessary to create invoices and sync products (customers, items, invoices, accounts).
  • Payment information: Subscription billing is processed by Stripe; we do not store credit card numbers.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To create invoices in your QuickBooks Online account when you instruct us to
  • To process subscription payments
  • To communicate with you about account, security, and product updates
  • To improve the Service

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Intuit / QuickBooks Online — when you authorize a connection, to create invoices and read products on your behalf.
  • Stripe — to process subscription payments.
  • Supabase — our hosting and database provider.
  • Service providers — only as necessary to operate the Service.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted database storage, encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), row-level security policies that isolate each customer's data, and rotating OAuth tokens for QuickBooks access.

Your Data Rights

You may export, modify, or delete your data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Disconnecting QuickBooks removes our access to your QBO account. Canceling your subscription removes your data after 30 days unless you request earlier deletion.

Cookies

We use only the cookies necessary to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Children

The Service is intended for use by businesses. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email.
