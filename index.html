Summary & roll-up

Add up hours and fund dollars across any span — one month, a quarter, a year, or a custom range of months. This is a records / reconciliation view, not an official form: the fund takes monthly remittances. Hours are bucketed by work month, so ranges snap to whole months.

Filing history

A saved record of each remittance you’ve filed. Tap Record filing on the Print / File tab after you submit one — it snapshots the hours, fund amounts and total as they were that day, so later edits to rates or hours never change the history. Stored on this device.

Contract update

When a new agreement takes effect, review it and enter what changed here — the app pushes the new premiums (fund rates), minimum hours, and limit (hours cap) into every report and the printed form, and logs the change with its effective date. Fields start at your current values, so you only touch what moved. (The app can’t read the contract for you — you key the numbers; it does the rest.)

Reporting rules
Payroll email — Tandem Tax
Pay period: by default the app hard-locks to the last Friday of each month — weeks run Sun–Sat and are paid the following Friday, so the period ends the Saturday before the month’s last Friday and the week count is the number of Fridays in the month (June 2026: last Friday the 26th → May 24 – Jun 20, 4 weeks). You don’t need to enter dates — they fill in automatically when you switch the work month. Type into the date boxes only to override a month; tap Auto to snap it back to the rule.
One-tap send: the Email payroll button on the Print / File tab opens your Mail app pre-addressed to Avery with everyone’s hours in the body. Send it from the vsaul@northerntireinc.com account so it comes from the right address. The app can’t attach files or auto-send on a schedule — to also send the forms, print them to PDF first and attach. On Sundays a reminder appears on the Print / File tab.

Basis: Per hour = rate × paid hours. % base wages = rate% of base wages (e.g., Vacation & Holiday). Flat = rate per active employee. Funds with a 0 rate don’t appear on the remittance. Mgmt: checked = this fund also applies to non-union / management employees (e.g., Health Care for Lee). Unchecked funds are union-only.
